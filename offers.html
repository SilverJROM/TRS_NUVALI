Offers

The Reading Station Nuvali offers specialized reading programs for children aged 3 to 13, focusing on personalized literacy development with dedicated teachers ensuring tailored guidance to build strong reading skills. This individualized attention creates a supportive environment where young learners grow confident while exploring books suited to their interests and reading level. By providing small-group or individual sessions, children learn at their own pace, accelerating progress without the delays of larger classroom settings, with a flexible, child-centered method that suits their unique needs and schedule. Located in the family-friendly Nuvali community, the station offers a nurturing space for kids to develop a lifelong love for reading.

How Successful is the Program?

The "Phono-graphics" program, introduced by American educators in the late 90s and adapted as the Phono-Graphics Reading Program (PGRP) for competitive schooling environments, is clinically researched, tested, and proven highly effective. Children completing the program read at third-grade level or higher. Hundreds of testimonials highlight students ranking on their schools' honor rolls, with special emphasis on decoding, encoding, comprehension, and fostering an early love for reading.

How Does the Program Work?

Tailored for young children, the Reading Station maximizes class hours through individual modules and modified teaching. The PGRP has 12 levels, with completion recommended for reading success. Children attend three 1-hour sessions weekly, requiring a minimum of 12 hours monthly.

For advanced learners, sessions are 1.5 hours twice weekly, also meeting the 12-hour monthly minimum.
