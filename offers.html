Offers

The Reading Station Nuvali offers specialized reading programs for children aged 3 to 13, focusing on personalized literacy development with dedicated teachers ensuring tailored guidance to build strong reading skills. This individualized attention, through one-on-one or one-to-two sessions, creates a supportive environment where young learners grow confident while exploring books suited to their interests and reading level. By providing small-group or individual sessions, children learn at their own pace, accelerating progress without the delays of larger classroom settings, with a flexible, child-centered method that suits their unique needs and schedule. Located in the family-friendly Nuvali community, the station offers a nurturing space for kids to develop a lifelong love for reading.

Our Programs

  • Reading (3-13 years old):
    A comprehensive program designed to enhance reading skills for children aged 3 to 13, offered both online and onsite. Features engaging, age-tailored activities to ignite a passion for reading and boost literacy confidence.
  • Basic Writing (10-12 years old):
    A foundational writing course for 10-12-year-olds, focusing on essential writing skills through interactive online and onsite sessions, building a strong base for creative expression.
  • Early Filipino (3-7 years old):
    An introductory program for 3-7-year-olds to master basic Filipino language skills onsite, featuring fun, educational activities to lay a solid foundation for early literacy.
  • Summer Package (New kids only):
    A special summer program for new students, packed with diverse reading and learning activities to kickstart their educational journey with excitement and growth.
  • Integrated Writing (9-12 years old):
    A holistic writing program for 9-12-year-olds, blending face-to-face and group sessions to enhance writing techniques and unleash creative potential.
  • Creative Writing (10-13 years old):
    An inspiring online course for 10-13-year-olds to explore imagination through creative writing, emphasizing portfolio development to showcase their unique talents.
